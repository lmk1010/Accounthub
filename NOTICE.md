## Privacy

Default self-host deployments store credentials, request metadata, and logs on **your** infrastructure.  
There is no requirement to send that data to AccountHub maintainers.  
You are the data controller for your deployment. Minimize logging of secrets and personal data; restrict admin access; use TLS at the edge.

## Experimental adapters

Some provider adapters are experimental or reverse-engineered from client behavior.  
They may break without notice and may not be permitted under provider terms.  
Prefer official APIs. Disable experimental channels if you do not accept that risk.
